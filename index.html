What We Do

Our Services

We offer a full range of professional painting services tailored to your home or business needs.

🏠

Interior Painting

Transform your living spaces with expert color consulting and flawless application. Bedrooms, living rooms, kitchens — we handle it all with minimal disruption to your daily routine.

🏗️

Exterior Painting

Boost your curb appeal and protect your home from New England's harsh weather. We pressure wash, prep, prime, and paint using weather-resistant coatings built to last.

🪵

Cabinet & Trim Painting

Give your kitchen or bathroom a fresh new look without a full renovation. Smooth, factory-like finishes on cabinets, doors, and trim.

🎨

Deck & Fence Staining

Protect and beautify your outdoor wood surfaces with premium stains and sealers. Extends the life of your deck and fence significantly through every season.

🚿

Power Washing

Deep cleaning for driveways, decks, siding, and fences. Our professional power washing removes dirt, mold, and stains, leaving your surfaces looking brand new across MA and NH.

🪚

Light Carpentry

From installing baseboards and crown molding to fixing damaged wood trim, we handle light carpentry repairs that complement our painting services for a perfect finish.
